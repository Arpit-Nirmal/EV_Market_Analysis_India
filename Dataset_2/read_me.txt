The electric vehicle (EV) market in India is burgeoning, with increasing interest and adoption among consumers. Understanding the dynamics and perceptions surrounding this market is crucial for stakeholders, including policymakers, manufacturers, and consumers themselves. Our aim is to delve into various aspects of the EV market in India, identifying key trends, preferences, and potential barriers to adoption. By analyzing the data, we seek to provide a comprehensive overview that will inform strategic decisions and foster a better understanding of consumer behavior towards EVs.
